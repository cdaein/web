<GC>
  Dae In Chung is an artist, designer and educator who mainly works with
  computer code as a creative medium. His main interests lie in exploring and
  expressing rhythms and movements using a variety of media, including
  animation, printed forms and interactive applications. He studied Experimental
  Animation at California Institute of the Arts. His design and animation work
  has been shown around the world including Centre Pompidou online screening, the New
  York Times, and many film and media festivals. He has also worked on various
  graphic design and motion graphics projects for commercial clients. His work
  has been recognized by Type Directors Club, Korean Society of Typography, IdN,
  CA Magazine Korea, PAGE Magazine, etc.

If you'd like to work with me, please [get in touch](mailto:daeinc@paperdove.com?subject=[Business%20Inquiry]).
